Meet Your Candidate

A Son of Bumasobo

Roots & Resilience

Born on 29th September 1994 in Bumasobo Buwakadala, Masuba Alvin is a true son of the soil. Born to Masuba Jacob, a respected community leader, and Nakato Sauya (Kainza Annet), he hails from the Bamatobole clan. Alvin knows first-hand the challenges of rural life, having walked the long, muddy roads to school. His upbringing instilled in him the values of hard work, community service, and integrity. Today, he combines that lived experience with legal expertise and a training in public management to advocate for the people of Elgon County.

Education Journey

Alvin's pursuit of knowledge has been driven by a desire to serve. From his early days at Fairway and Grace Primary School to his legal studies at IUIU, every step has prepared him for leadership. He believes that education is the great equalizer and is committed to ensuring every child in Elgon County has the same opportunities.
